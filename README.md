# sqrtl calendar birthdays

this patch adds recurring birthdays to the calendar.

## changed

- birthdays appear every year as:
  - bob day — jan 9
  - ant day — jan 10
  - mario day — apr 19
  - jus day — may 29
  - erp day — nov 29
  - ame day — dec 17
- birthday labels use a pink style
- existing BC/Richmond holidays stay included
